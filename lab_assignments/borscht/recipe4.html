perfect borscht

Serves 4

melt 50g of butter large pan

soften a small onion over a gentle heat (5 minutes)

Add 1 carrot

Add 1 leek

Add 1 celery,

Add 1 diced beetroot

Add allspice and bay leaf

Grate 75 g of beetroot

Pour stock and potatoes

Simmer for 15 minutes

Add shredded ½ small cabbage

Add 4 peeled and crushed cloves of garlic

Add Grated beetroot

Cook until vegetables are tender (10 minutes)

Add 2 tsp cider vinegar

Add 1 tsb sugar

Add ½ tsp ground black pepper

Add Pinch of salt and taste

Serve with Dollop of sour cream

Add Sprig of dill

Serve with Polish bread
