The Perfect Borscht!!

(Serves 4)

  1. melt 50g of butter large pan

  2. soften a small onion over a gentle heat (5 minutes)

  3. Add 1 carrot

  4. Add 1 leek

  5. Add 1 celery

  6. Add 1 diced beetroot

  7. Add allspice and bay leaf

  8. Grate 75 g of beetroot

  9. Pour stock and potatoes

  10. Simmer for 15 minutes

  11. Add shredded ½ small cabbage

  12. Add 4 peeled and crushed cloves of garlic

  13. Add Grated beetroot

  14. Cook until vegetables are tender (10 minutes)

  15. Add 2 tsp cider vinegar

  16. Add 1 tsb sugar

  17. Add ½ tsp ground black pepper

  18. Add Pinch of salt and taste

  19. Serve with Dollop of sour cream

  20. Add Spring of dill

  21. serve with Polish bread
