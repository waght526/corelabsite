Recipe for the Perfect Borscht!!

Perfect borscht
(Serves 4)

Melt 50g of butter large pan
soften a small onion over a gentle heat (5 minutes)

Add the following and stir well so that they’re coated in butter (10 mins)-
1 carrot
1 leek
1 celery,
1 diced beetroot
allspice and bay leaf

After this-
Grate 75 g of beetroot
Pour stock and potatoes
Simmer for 15 minutes

Add the following-
Add shredded ½ small cabbage
4 peeled and crushed cloves of garlic
Grated beetroot
Cook until vegetables are tender (10 minutes)

Add the following-
2 tsp cider vinegar
1 tsb sugar
½ tsp ground black pepper
Pinch of salt and taste

Serve with-
Dollop of sour cream
Sprig of dill
Polish bread
